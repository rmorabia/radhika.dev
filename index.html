About Me

I'm a 17-year-old from the suburbs of Los Angeles, California and I'm obsessed with achieving success.

Just a few years ago, I was a top student aiming to go to one of the top 30 universities in the US. In my Freshman year of high school, I injured myself by following the typical high school advice to do everything. After months of seeing doctors and failing to find a solution, I eventually had to leave my high school. My life trajectory seemed ruined.

I was dragged through alternative school after alternative school all while struggling to handle the constant pain my injury gave me. I eventually decided to veer off the path entirely. Since then, I've been working on creating opportunities for myself and learning the secrets to success.

I began networking with people I admire, I started a consulting business, and I've been reading lots of books. I'm currently running a business to help web designers find the clients of their dreams and am learning digital strategy in the hopes of eventually joining a firm. Hire me?
